Album Title: Student Council Ceremony 2025
  • << Change Album
  • Event date: 09/05/2025
  • Updated on: 26/05/2025
  • Total Photos: 440
Description: Leadership is not about power, its about responsibility. Its about inspiring and serving others with integrity and dedication. The Investiture ceremony for session 2025-26 was held on 9 May, 2025.It was a significant event marking the beginning of a new Academic and leadership chapter. The ceremony began with the oath administered by Fr. Principal. The newly elected leaders pledged to uphold the values and responsibility entrusted to them. Badges, symbol of authority and trust were presented to Students Council of std 9& 10 by Mr. Amar Prakash and Fr Vice Principal. Then the flags were handed over to the President by Mr. Yogendra Dubey and to the captains by Mr. Sanjeev Kumar. It was time to then present badges to the young leaders of the primary wing by Mr. Amar Prakash. These young leaders were filled with zest and vigour to fulfilthe responsibilities entrusted to them. Leadership is best learned through experience and wisdom. Anmol Shankar, the President inspired our new leaders with his words and shared the vision for this Academic year Every successful event is the result of teamwork and dedication. Sidrah Rahman, the Head Girl proposed vote of thanks. As the memorable Investiture Ceremony came to a close, the selfless dedication of our outgoing Students Council was acknowledged and we were fulled with hope and anticipation for the journey our newly elected Students Council members were about to undertake. The event closed with the National Anthem.
